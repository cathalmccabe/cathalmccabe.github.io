Academic Programs and projects

XUP provides free free training material and workshops for academics, and a donation program for Xilinx hardware, software and IP.
NetFPGA is an open-source research project focused on network applications. NetFPGA boards are available at a subsidized price for teaching and research.
ROACH (Reconfigurable Open Architecture Computing Hardware), is an FPGA processing platform designed for the CASPER astronomy project.
