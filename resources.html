Useful webpages

Xilinx University Program (XUP)

XUP provides free hardware, and Xilinx licenses for software and IP for teaching and research. Donation requests can be made through the website. The XUP also has free training material (presentations and labs) that can be used for self learning, or modified/reused for classroom teaching.

Digilent

Digilent provide low cost Xilinx FPGA and Zynq development boards intended for students, universities, and pro-makers.

NetFPGA

The NetFPGA is an open source research community focused on network applications. NetFPGA boards are available at a subsidized price for teaching and research.

Roach Project

ROACH (Reconfigurable Open Architecture Computing Hardware), is an FPGA processing platform designed for the astronomy community.
