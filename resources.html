Conferences Xilinx University Program (XUP) The Xilinx University Program supports teaching and research using the latest Xilinx tools and technologies. Donation request for Xilinx software and IP, Educational boards can be made through the website. Also available is a range of training material (presentations and labs) that can be modified/reused directly for classroom teaching. Digilent Digilent Inc. is a leading provider of FPGA and SOC products serving students, universities, and OEM's worldwide with technology-based educational design tools. NetFPGA The NetFPGA is a line-rate, flexible, and open platform for research, and classroom experimentation. More than 2,000 NetFPGA systems have been deployed at over 150 institutions in over 40 countries around the world. Roach Project ROACH (Reconfigurable Open Architecture Computing Hardware), is a standalone FPGA processing platform for the astronomy community.
