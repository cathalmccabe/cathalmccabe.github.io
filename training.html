Seminars and training events

Avnet

Avnet regularly provide Xilinx themed seminars, workshops, and webinars.
www.em.avnet.com/en-us/design/trainingandevents/

Doulos

Doulos, a Xilinx ATP often hold free 1-hour technical webinars on a range of EDA topics. The webinars usually offer a very good introduction to a topic and can be followed up by attended a Doulos training course.
www.doulos.com/content/training/webinars.php
